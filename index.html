Percussionist

An avid percussionist since 2006, I've had the pleasure of learning from and performing with some of the best artists in the world. Currently I play the Dumbek, Daf, Tombek and Dayereh. I will always think of myself as a student and will never lose the desire to learn, expand my skills, and grow as a musician. For me it's about the journey, not the end result. These days you can catch me around Toronto drumming with Suleiman Warwar and the Arabesque Dance Company & Orchestra, as well as the Sarghi Percussion Ensemble led by Naghmeh Farahmand.

Some places I've had the pleasure of performing in: Fleck Dance Theater, Canadian Opera Company, CNE Exibition World Stage, Taste of Danforth, Queen's Park for Canada Day, Beit Zatoun.
