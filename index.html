Bio

Freelance photographer since 2007, me and my trusty Nikon D300 have captured many beautiful images together. Throughout the years I've dabbled in wedding photography, studio shoots with dancers and models, martial arts shoots, babies, pets and travel photography. My real passion however, lies in the field of dance photography and capturing movement in a photo.

Feel free to scroll right and take a look at some of my favorite photos that I've captured over the years and contact me if you have a project in mind that I would be perfect for.

Special shout out goes to Arabesque Dance Company for giving me the opportunity to take many of these beautiful images.
